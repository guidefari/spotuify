Notes:
- Confirm modal: RED title chip with `⚠`, button chips `y · yes`
  (Danger=RED) and `n · no` (Cancel=CHIP_BG), inline `Esc cancel`
  hint.
- Error modal: title icon adapts to the error category — 🔒 for
  auth/403, ⚡ for 411 (the "internal bug" case), ✖ for 5xx, 📡
  for network blips, ⚠ for everything else. The hint line below the
  body explains the recovery, then the footer carries `Esc dismiss`
  + `? help`.
- Playlist picker: focused card (GREEN border), ● for selected
  rows, ○ for unselected, cursor `▌` for the focused row.
