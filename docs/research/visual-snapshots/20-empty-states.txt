Step 20 snapshot — Empty states
Every per-screen empty state now leads with a throbber + bold copy +
muted clarification, or an actionable prompt with GREEN accent.

Library (empty + auto-sync in progress):
 ⠋  Fetching your library…
 It refreshes automatically and stays cached.

Library (auto-sync finished, still 0 items):
 Your library will appear here.
 It refreshes automatically once auth completes. Press u to force a refresh.

Search (idle):
 Ready to search.
 Press / and type an artist, song, album, or playlist.

Search (in-flight):
 ⠋  Searching Spotify and local cache…
 Local matches surface first; remote results stream in.

Queue (empty):
 Queue is empty.
 Press `e` on any track or album to enqueue.

Devices (empty):
 No visible devices
 Open Spotify on a phone/laptop/speaker to make it visible. Press u to refresh.

Diagnostics (loading):
 ⠋  Loading doctor…
 Auto-fetching the daemon report, cache stats, and recent logs.

Lyrics (no synced lyrics):
 No synced lyrics for this track.
 (some tracks are instrumental, or the provider doesn't have them.)

Lyrics (loading):
 ⠋  Fetching synced lyrics…
 Spotify provider first, LRCLIB fallback.

Playlist drill-in (loading tracks):
 ⠋  Loading tracks…
 Press b to go back.

Notes:
- Spinner animates at ~12 fps using the `⠋⠙⠹⠸⠼⠴⠦⠧⠇⠏` braille sequence.
- All empty states say what's happening AND what to do next, never
  just "no data."
- "Run spotuify sync library" / "press u to fetch" prompts are gone
  from the user-facing copy (auto-sync handles those).
